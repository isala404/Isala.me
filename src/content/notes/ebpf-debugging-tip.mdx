---
title: "Quick eBPF debugging tip"
publishedAt: 2024-11-08
tags: ["ebpf", "debugging"]
---

When your eBPF program silently fails, check the verifier logs:

```bash
bpftool prog load myprogram.o /sys/fs/bpf/myprogram verbose
```

The `verbose` flag dumps the verifier's thought process. Usually reveals why your program was rejected faster than staring at the code.
